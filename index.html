    DJmag top 100 DJs

    The results of all the DJmag top 100 DJs competitions from 1997 until today.

    • Live running version

    History

    The DJmag top 100 poll was instituted in 1991. Back then it was just an alphabetical list of the best 100 DJ's. In the year 1997 it became a ranking for all the DJ's in the world. The results before 1997 are not included because not all of the information is presently available.

    API

    The following routes are available:

    GET /years/:year

    The ranking of the specific year.

    GET /artists/:artist

    The ranking of the artist through the years.
